Our Visionary Leadership

Founded in 2018 by Martin Nyaga, Lifehack Academy was born from a simple observation: every child is a unique genius waiting for the right environment.

Under Martin's leadership, we have grown from a small tuition center into a premier international school that blends academic rigor with the emotional intelligence required for modern success.

"We don't just teach subjects; we coach life skills. Our training ensures holistic growth that stays with a student long after they leave the classroom."

Martin Nyaga

Director & Lead Educator
